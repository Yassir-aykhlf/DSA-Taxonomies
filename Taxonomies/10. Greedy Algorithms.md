> **Related Patterns**:
> - Dynamic Programming (the fallback when a local choice is not provably optimal)
> - Heap & Priority Queue (serves the best-available element for priority greedies)
> - Sorting (most greedy proofs start from a sorted key)
> - Union Find (Kruskal's greedy MST unions the cheapest safe edge)
