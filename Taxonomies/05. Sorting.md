> **Related Patterns**:
> - Divide & Conquer (merge and quick sort recurse then combine)
> - Heap (priority-ordered extraction for top-K and streaming selection)
> - Two Pointers (the merge sweep and quicksort partition are pointer moves)
> - Greedy (most greedy proofs open on a sorted order)
