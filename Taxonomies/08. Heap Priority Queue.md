> **Related Patterns**:
> - Greedy Algorithms (a heap supplies the always-take-the-best choice)
> - Traversal Algorithms (Dijkstra and Prim relax from a heap frontier)
> - Sorting (a heap gives partial order for top-K without a full sort)
> - Two Pointers (an alternative when the data is already sorted)
