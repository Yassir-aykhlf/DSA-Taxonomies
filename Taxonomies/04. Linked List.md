> **Related Patterns**:
> - Two Pointers (fast/slow and runner alignment walk the nodes)
> - Recursion (mirrors the pointer unwinding of list operations)
> - Merge Sort (stable node merging underlies list sorting)
> - Hash Table (original-to-copy maps clone lists with side pointers)
