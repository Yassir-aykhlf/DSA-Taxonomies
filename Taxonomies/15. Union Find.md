> **Related Patterns**:
> - Traversal Algorithms (DFS/BFS is the alternative on a static graph)
> - Sorting (Kruskal sorts edges before unioning them)
> - Heap & Priority Queue (Prim builds the same MST from a heap frontier)
> - Hash Table (maps arbitrary labels to dense DSU indices)
