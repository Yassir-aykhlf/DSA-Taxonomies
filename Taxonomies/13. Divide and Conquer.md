> **Related Patterns**:
> - Dynamic Programming (D&C with cached overlapping subproblems)
> - Binary Search (the simplest D&C — discard half each step)
> - Sorting (merge and quick sort are the canonical divide-and-conquer sorts)
> - Recursion (the control structure every D&C algorithm rides on)
