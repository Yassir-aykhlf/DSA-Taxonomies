> **Related Patterns**:
> - Heap & Priority Queue (the priority-ordered specialization of a queue)
> - Traversal Algorithms (a queue drives BFS; a stack drives iterative DFS)
> - Sliding Window (a monotonic deque holds the window extreme in O(1) amortized)
> - Two Pointers (an index pair replaces a stack for some linear scans)
