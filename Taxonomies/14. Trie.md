> **Related Patterns**:
> - String Manipulation (tries excel at prefix-heavy string work)
> - Traversal Algorithms (DFS/BFS walk a trie's nodes)
> - Backtracking (a trie prunes dead branches in grid/word search)
> - Bit Manipulation (a binary trie solves maximum-XOR bit by bit)
