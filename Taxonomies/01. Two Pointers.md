> **Related Patterns**:
> - Binary Search (a midpoint check decides which pointer to move)
> - Sliding Window (a same-direction specialization for contiguous spans)
> - Sorting (the merge step is a two-pointer sweep; k-sum sorts first)
> - Linked List (cycle, middle, and nth-from-end all ride fast/slow pointers)
