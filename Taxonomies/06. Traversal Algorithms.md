> **Related Patterns**:
> - Union Find (an alternative for connectivity on a static graph)
> - Backtracking (grid path search is DFS that unmarks on the way back)
> - Heap & Priority Queue (Dijkstra and Prim pull the cheapest frontier node)
> - Dynamic Programming (many DP recurrences follow a fixed traversal order)
