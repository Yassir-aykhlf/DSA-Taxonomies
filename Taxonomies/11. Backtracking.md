> **Related Patterns**:
> - Traversal Algorithms (backtracking is DFS that undoes each choice)
> - Dynamic Programming (memoizing overlapping subproblems turns it into DP)
> - Recursion (the structural foundation every backtracker builds on)
> - Bit Manipulation (a bitmask compactly tracks used elements or board state)
