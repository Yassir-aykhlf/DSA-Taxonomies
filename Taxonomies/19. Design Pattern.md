> **Related Patterns**:
> - Hash Table (the workhorse inside most custom structures)
> - Heap & Priority Queue (backs slot pools, leaderboards, and feed merges)
> - Trie (powers filesystem and autocomplete designs)
> - Linked List (a doubly linked list underlies eviction caches and history)
