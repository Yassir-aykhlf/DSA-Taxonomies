> **Related Patterns**:
> - Recursion (DP is a recurrence plus a cache)
> - Greedy Algorithms (replaces DP when a local choice is provably optimal)
> - Backtracking (exhaustive search that becomes DP once memoized)
> - Divide & Conquer (splits too, but DP reuses overlapping subproblems)
