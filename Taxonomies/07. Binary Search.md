> **Related Patterns**:
> - Two Pointers (converging indices decide a half without a mid-point)
> - Divide & Conquer (binary search is its discard-half special case)
> - Sorting (establishes the monotonic order binary search relies on)
> - Heap (an alternative route to Kth-value and top-K answers)
